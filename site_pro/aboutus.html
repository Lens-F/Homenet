About us

Homenet was established in 1993 and has been serving the New York
tri-state area for the past 19 years. We are an owner operated business that takes pride in our customer's complete satisfaction. We specialize in complete home automation solutions which include the integration of lighting control, security, home theater, audio/video, communications, networking and structured wiring. This technology can be controlled locally and remotely.


Ayal Noah, president of Homenet has over 25 years of experience with electronics and technology.  Homenet works with the most cutting edge technology and can design the "smart" home of your dreams! Our goal is to provide our customers with the most user friendly technology.

 

 

 

 

 

 

This is how we work:
Step 1: We begin each job by listening to what our client needs and would like to have. We set up a meeting where we can discuss these needs and what is available within the technology and the client’s budget.
Step 2:  We will then create a detailed proposal outlining the cost of the job with a breakdown of all materials and services. If necessary we will meet again with the client to discuss the proposal.
Step 3: After the proposal is signed and approved we then begin work.  The owner is always on premises and working directly with the client to ensure that the job gets done on time and as planned.
