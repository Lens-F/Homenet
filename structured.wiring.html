    Structured wiring

    Homenet will replace conventional TV, phone and computer wiring with “structured wiring”, an improved wiring infrastructure that will insure that you will be able to take advantage of today’s technology while easily allowing you to adapt your System for all future digital and communication technologies. Communication wiring- networking is at the center of what is needed to enable your home to maximize the full performance and bandwidth of high-speed internet.This will provide clear reception of the new digital and HDTV signal throughoutthe home. This structured wiring also allows for multiple phone lines and options for telephone/intercom systems. Data wiring with in the main structured wiring of your home will allow for hard-wired computer networks which creates wider bandwidths and faster transmission speeds that wireless networks alone cannot provide. This makes transferring music files and streaming video so much faster!
